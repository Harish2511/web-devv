Safety Guidelines

If COVID-19 is spreading in your community, stay safe by taking some simple precautions listed below:

    • Every individual should wear a mask in public.
    • Even if you have a mask on, maintain social distancing.
    • If someone in your household is infected, everyone should wear mask to prevent the virus from spreading.
    • Wash your hands with soap or sanitize them before putting on a mask.
    • Make sure your nose and mouth are completely covered by the mask.

    • In your home, avoid contact with sick people.
    • If possible, keep distance from the sick person as often as possible.
    • If you have to step out, put a 1 meter distance between yourself and others.
    • It is possible that someone without symptoms is a carrier.

    • As soon as possible, get a date fixed and get an authorised vaccination.
    • Vaccine with protect you from COVID-19, and you will be able to do some tasks That had to be stopped before pandemic

    • Being in crowded public places puts you at a higher risk of getting COVID-19
    • Similarly, you are at risk if you are in an enclosed space where the virus may linger.
    • Avoid crowds as much as possible and stay in well ventilated rooms.

    • Wash your hands as frequently as possible with soap and water.
    • If soap and water are not available, use a hand sanitizer with 60% alcohol content.
