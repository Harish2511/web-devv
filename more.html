Here are the basics of how to wear a mask:

  1. Make sure it covers both your nose, mouth and chin.
  2. Clean your hands before you put your mask on, as well as before and after you take it off, and after you touch it at any time.
  3. When you take off a mask, store it in a clean plastic bag, and every day either wash it if it’s a fabric mask, or dispose of a medical mask in a trash bin.
  4. Don’t use masks with valves.

A Mask must contain three layers for efficient protection
