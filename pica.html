Mujeebur Rahman, a member of Tamil Nadu Muslim Muneetra Kazhagam (TNMMK) has been working tirelessly to help those
affected by Covid-19. From catering to the needs of patients to helping families perform last rites of their dear ones, he
and his team members have left no stone unturned in giving back to the community.

He and his team members have been providing free hearse service and funeral service to Covid victims whose families
cannot afford the unreasonable rates demanded by ambulance owners.

"There are a lot of expenses involved in a Covid burial. There won’t be manpower to take the body, it has
to be done using JCB. Cotton cloth has to be used in large numbers. People involved in the burial have to be
provided with a PPE kit. Some families pay for these expenses but some can’t afford it, so we seek help
from those who can provide assistance," Rahman said.
